The Auction Process

1. Registration

Download Form or visit MFC branch at 5 Lindsay Street, Esselen Park, Thembisa, 1626 after getting your buyers code.

  • • Provide FICA documents (ID, Proof of Residence).
  • • Pay refundable 20% of the buyer code.
  • • Receive Buyers Code (Required for entry).

2. "Buy Now" Catalogue

Select vehicles are available for immediate purchase. Contact the Finance Manager to view the fixed-price ZAR catalogue (VAT included).

3. Weekly Auctions

Held weekly. Viewing by appointment only. Bidding via floor (with Buyers Code) or online via MFC portal. Highest bid above reserve wins.

4. FAQ & Finance

Can I finance? Yes, Nedbank consultants are available on-site.

Are cars tested? Yes, scheduled DEKRA reports are available.
