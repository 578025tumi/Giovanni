Executive Biography

David Knight is an international automotive entrepreneur and vehicle auction specialist with extensive experience in the global motor industry. His professional background spans online vehicle auctions, automotive finance, fleet management, vehicle sourcing, dealer operations, and international vehicle trading across the United States, Canada, Europe, Australia, South Africa, and the United Kingdom. Throughout his career, Giovanni has worked with vehicle finance institutions, dealership networks, auction platforms, and private automotive investors, building strong expertise in both retail and wholesale vehicle markets. His leadership experience includes managing large-scale automotive operations, vehicle remarketing, cross-border vehicle logistics, and digital auction solutions.
Giovanni later founded a modern online vehicle auction platform focused on secure transactions, trusted vehicle listings, transparent bidding systems, and customer-centered automotive services. He holds a Bachelor of Commerce (Finance) Degree from the University of Calgary (Canada).
